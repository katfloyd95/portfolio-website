FAQ

What services do you offer?

I work specifically in Shopify development. Services include customized Shopify store setup, store migrations, theme edits, and any store bugs that may need fixing.

How do you charge per project?

Depending on the project, I will quote a price upfront after discussing the services that you are looking for. That way you will know exactly how much you are paying for when working with me. For any ongoing projects such as web maintenance, I charge hourly. The hourly price can be negotiated in our first call.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes! If you are looking for someone to maintain your Shopify store, we can arrange that.

How will the discovery call be structured?

The purpose of the discovery call is to discuss any upfront questions that you might have, and whether there are any services you might need. Depending on the services you are looking for, I will recommend an approach and some ideas on how to get the job done. Finally, we will negotiate a cost for the services that works for both of us.

What is the typical timeline for a project?

It depends on the extent of the project. Some will take less than a week and others may require months. The best way to find out is to book a free call with me, and we can discuss it!
